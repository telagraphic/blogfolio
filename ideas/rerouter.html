Re-Router

2020

Pitch

A protocol with a philosophy

The Idea

Upgrade your router with an easy app-like interface for controlling your security/privacy tools and balance your internet use with features that track and limit your screen time. There are too many security tools for each device, too many sites that trap our attention, Re-Router will cover 90% of your needs and put you back in charge.

Concepts

Security and privacy are obvious problems in today's connected but so has our mental and physical health with too much screen time, social media addiction and non-stup 24/7/365 stimulation at the touch of our fingers. Our brains aren't wired to handle the information overload, neither can it handle all the steps required to protect our identity and data on our devices.

Don't hassle with setting up security tools on your devices, upgrade the one you already use - the router! With open source technology (ad blocker, dns, and VPN) you can cover most of you security needs. Track and limit your internet use with easy site blocking playlists, password manager, internet off/on scheduler with a nice app like interface.

That thing that sits in the corner of your closet or hallway is actually the wide open front door to the Internet. Almost all routers lack anything that is easy or nice to look at it, you get in and get out when you have internet problems. Re-Router provides an app-like experience with a simple user interface that offers features that go beyond boring network details.

ReRouter simplifies all the layers of web security and makes it easey to setup and control with the best open source software. The Internet of today is so very different than the internet of 10 years ago. Social media, Youtube, Netflix and 24/7/365 stimulating content at the touch of finger has changed our minds and way of life.

Re-Router gives control back to the conscious tech user and provides a set of tools and features to adjust your internet habits.

Re-Router, Modern Modem

Security/Privacy Features

Surf safer with open-source security tools to block ads, prevent your ISP from snooping on your traffic and browsing the web anonymously.

  • PiHole Ad Blocker
  • OpenDNS integration
  • PiVPN
  • Password manager
  • Basic security defaults built in that are usually disabled and not-understandable to average users
  • Easy device management for the above security services

Digital Minimalist Features

Control your internet use with the ability to schedule when your router should turn on and off, create site blocking playlists that block distracting sites when you work.

  • On/Off time-based scheduler to limit internet use
  • Create site blocker 'playlists' for work and play
  • Traffic tracking to see your browsing rabbit holes and time waster
  • Browser app or plugin to control the above controls
