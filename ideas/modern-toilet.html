Toilet 2.0

2022

Pitch

Squat Ergonomics + Exhaust Fan + Flush Scenter + Bidet = Proper Way to #2

The Idea

A modern toilet design for natural squatting form, odor exhaust fans, scent perfume on flush and bidet option.

Domain Names

  • moderntoilet.com

Alternate Names

  • Modern Toilet
  • Toilet 2.0

Features/Content

  • Foot positions and hand rails for safe and comfortable squat positioning
  • 360 degree full bowl exhaust fan pulls odors out before they spread out
  • Each flush releases a water based scent perfume that kills bacteria and removes odor
  • Bidet option provides a water based alternative to toilet paper
  • One peice curved design with no hard edges or flat sides makes cleaning a breeze

Concepts

A toilet designed for proper anatomical squatting form, an exhaust fan to remove odors before they spread around and scent release after each flush, along with a bidet option for a fresh natural clean.
