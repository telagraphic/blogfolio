Hi Grinder

2020

Pitch

Automatic Grinder + Bluetooth Speaker + USB Vape Charger + Color LED Light + 4:20 Reminder + Weed Smart Assitant

The Idea

Upgrade your weed grinder with an automatic mechanized unit that plays music and charges your devices and vape pens. Set a reminder for 4:20 everyday and have your weed ready to roll or pack. LED color light sets the mood for late nite parties.

Domain Names

  • higrinder.com

Features/Content

  • App that pairs with the grinder hardware for grinder, music playing and alarm setting
  • Can grind weed into a cone right after or dispense into a bowl or bong.
  • Play music from your phone to get the party started
  • Pair with your voice assistant to order ride share serivces
  • Ask questions to the smart assistant about weed strains and current events news
