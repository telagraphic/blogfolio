Phone Fairy

2020

Pitch

Phone + Locker + "Lock It Up" = Phone Fairy

The Idea

A locker to put your #1 digital distraction on hold so that you can get your work done. A practical way to build up "phone-distancing" and better digital habits to live a better life.

Concepts

One of the best ways to prevent phone distraction is to put it in another room. An even better way is to "lock it up". The phone fairy is a sleep case that is the perfect new home for your phone or tablet. Top of the case has digital timer and lock for putting your phone on timeout while you work or live life. Comes with a physical key for manual locking as well.

Comes with standard electrical plug to charge your phone when in case. Optional feature of faraday caging to prevent any network communication from getting in or out. Also a great travel case too.

Your phone can even distract you when not using.

Features

The locker for your phone.

  • Built in charger
  • Faraday caging to prevent requests getting in/out
  • Digital and physical lock and timer
