Hi Phone

2020

Pitch

An open source phone powered by HiOS and featuring apps and hardware for the everyday smoker.

The Idea

Packing features like a joint holder, accelerometer controlled lighter, scale screen and THC image sensor along with standard smart phone features.

Domain Names

  • hiphone.com

Features/Content

  • Joint holder on back on phone for discrete storage
  • Use your screen as a scale
  • Lighter on top of phone controlled by accelerometer
  • Sophisticated image detection and analysis for strain identification and analysis
  • Plug In to add-ons like Hi Grinder and Hi Scale

Concepts

Can buy the fake phone outright or mod your existing phone with physical add-ons and installing the HiOS

Augmented Reality app to help roll the perfect joint
