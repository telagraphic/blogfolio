At Maps

2020

Pitch

Multi-Search + Connect The Dots = At Maps

The Idea

Perform multiple searches in parallel and display them all to easily connect your next spots. Removes the pain of deleting previous searches for "dance clubs" to then search for "dive bars".

Domain Names

  • atmaps.com
  • spotdotwalk

Alternate Names

  • Map Stack
  • Spot Dot Walk
  • Dot Spot
  • Spot Plot
  • Map Spot

Features/Content

  • Toggle between searches
  • Overlay all searches and connect your spots between your searches
  • Save favorite locations
  • Customize map colors
  • Emoji icons categories for the search icons
  • Share your hitlists with others

Concepts

A really easy and simple way to visualize multiple location searches for finding the next spot to jump to. Great for when your out on the town and need to choose between many options.

Most map apps only allow you to search for one thing at a time in sequence, you can't search for cocktails bars, movie theaters and dance clubs on the same map. At Maps allows multiple searches in parallel, allowing you to toggle hide/show locations from a specific search with your other searches.

Copy

"Plot, dot, and walk."
