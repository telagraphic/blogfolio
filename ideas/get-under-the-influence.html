The Idea

Pro-drug PSA's that highlight the differences between use, mis-use and abuse that emphasize responsible drug taking. A secondary theme of tech use, social media, pornography, and other related subjects draws a comparison between behavioral addictions to chemical ones. Brain science in addiction, habit formation and psyschology are emphasized.
