Anti Texting & Driving PSA

2020

Pitch

Car + Megaphone + Large Posters + Social Media = Shaming Drivers Who Text

The Idea

Car patrols use megaphones, large poster signs and social media to shame texting while driving

Domain Names

  • textanddie.com

Alternate Names

  • Text and Die

Concepts

Print out large signs, get group of people into a car, drive around filming people on phones and shun them with signs, honking and loudspeakers.

The passengers show their sign on the windows, or stick them out and wave to flag the distracted driver. The license plate # is taken down and put up on twitter for public shaming.

Posters with emoji's and short messages would be waived to distract the driver from their phone while driving.

Car could be fitted with external or window placed speakers wired up to a cell phone, passenger speaks into the phone reciting phone use and driving. Car horn funny sound effect could be used to flag drivers attention, horn sound replaced with cell phone sound effect.
