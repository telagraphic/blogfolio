NSA My Way

2020

Pitch

App that works with NSA database servers to share your data and spy tools for personal use

Concept

The idea is to get support for a White House Petition that would open up an API to the NSA database servers that collect our data and share it with any citizen with a social security number. By showcasing NSA internet and device exploits as 'cool features' within the app, we could provide valuable and helpful services to users.

The website would be modeled like an apple product page highlighting the features and how you could use them. At the bottom of the page is the form/link to the petition to help get our app allowed to work with government servers.

The app business model would be some sort of non-profit/public-private partnership with the government to share it's data and tech tools with it's taxpaying citizens.
