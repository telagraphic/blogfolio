Productivity Paper

2020

Pitch

Paper based productivity templates for a modular analogue approach to getting stuff done

The Idea

A productivity "framework/approach" that emphasizes time tested mental models and hands on paper workflow. Instead of a huge system, individual templates allow you build an approach that works for you. Instead of using an app, put the principles into practice and grow the skill of thinking and working smarter.

Concepts

There is too much productivity porn out there! Instead of learning new tricks and applying mental models in haphazard fashion, how about a simple approach that will cover 80% of your needs and use cases? It's super simple, get some wall space, print out some productivity models and start figuring out the best way to get your projects done.

Being "productive" can be considered a skill and you'll need tools in the form of models and concepts to apply to your everyday tasks and projects.

Models/Concepts take a productivity idea like 80/20 or SMART and break it down to the granular how-to of putting it into practice with examples. The real power is to use one model to think through another!

Using a digital workflow has it's strengths but using a paper analogue approach will make you strengthen your thinking about your work and projects. Building up your own system will fit your needs and help you rather than trying to fit into someone else's box.

At the very least, some of the models will be convenient reminders on your desk or wall, at most it can assist you with tackling your work smarter and harder.
