Light Fan

2021

Pitch

Vornado Fans + Color LED Light + Air Scenter + Bluetooth Speaker + App = Light Fan

The Idea

Upgrade your small depressing apartment with a dual vornado fan box with smart LED color lights along with a scent mister controlled by an app.

Domain Names

  • lightfan.com

Alternate Names

  • Phone Locker

Features/Content

  • App that controls fan direction, speed, light colors and scent mister
  • Dual vornado fans that circulate air throughout the room with low to high speeds and direction control
  • LED color lights for dope lighting
  • Bluetooth speaker for music pairing with your device
  • Scent mister that sprays into the fan for easy room coverage, great for smokers and dirty people
  • Easy to install for small spaces

Concepts

Most fans suck, they are based on 80's technology. Vornado fans use vorticular rotation to spread airflow throughout the room. You can also control the direction with 90 degree positioning.

Add some color to your drab space with two LED lights to party better and boost your mood on low light days.

Freshen up the stale air with a natural scent mister that is spread around by the fans, just a touch and not too much

Optional bluetooth speaker for music from above, the best way for full room audio coverage

All controlled by an easy to use app, can pair up to 3 light-fan units on a network
